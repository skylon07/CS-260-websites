Who am I?

My name is Amy Welter. I've been taking pictures since age 13, and I love being a professional photographer! I love capturing the essence of beauty for everyone to enjoy. And the only thing I love more than taking pictures of nature is taking pictures of you!

I graduated from Utah Valley University with a degree in Media Communication, but my photography story starts back in 2009 when I fell in love with taking pictures of the floweres in my backyard. Now I've fallen in love with taking pictures of beauty wherever I can find it!

If you are interested in me helping you save your special moments, please get in touch with me. I'd love to capture your moment with you!
